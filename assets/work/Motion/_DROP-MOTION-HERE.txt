MOTION page — how it works:

1) HERO REEL (plays big at the top, autoplays muted):
   Drop ONE short video file here, named starting with 01- (e.g. 01-reel.mp4).
   Keep it small (ideally under ~40 MB) since it's hosted on the site directly.

2) EVERYTHING ELSE = YouTube (fast, no size limits):
   Upload your videos to your YouTube channel, then paste the links into
   "videos.txt" in this folder — one per line. They show as a clickable grid
   below the hero reel and play when clicked.

Why: full-size video files are too large for the website host (GitHub caps
files at 100 MB), so long/large videos live on YouTube and the site embeds them.
