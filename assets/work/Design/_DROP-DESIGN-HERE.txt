Drop your DESIGN (graphic design) work in this folder.

Everything you put here appears automatically on the DESIGN page, in filename order.
Images: .jpg .png .webp .gif .svg   •   Videos: .mp4 .webm .mov
PDFs:   .pdf  --> every page is turned into an image and shown (great for
        multi-page brochures, layouts, decks). Just drop the PDF in and publish.

Tip: name files 01-name, 02-name... to control the order.
(For a PDF, its pages appear as 01-name-p01, 01-name-p02, ... automatically.)
